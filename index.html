About Joey

Joey and Baseball

Joey Eamigh has been working with the Carmel Christian Cougars Baseball team to help them improve their game with technology and statistics. In his words, "I am the Baseball Analytics Manager for the Carmel Christain Cougars Baseball team. I use a device called the Flight Scope Strike to gather data on the team and then analyze it in a dashboard that I built in Microsoft PowerBI. This piece of technology allows me to get real-time, accurate statistics, and use them to analyze our team and see how we need to improve. This technology has already taken us to the state championship in the first year of use, and I cannot wait to see where it takes us in the future!" Learn More
